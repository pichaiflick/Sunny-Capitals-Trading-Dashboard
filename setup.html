Welcome — Step 1 of 4

Set up your trading dashboard

Connect your Notion trading journal to get live stats, charts, AI coaching and more. Takes about 5 minutes.

1
Duplicate the Notion template — open the Trading Journal template and click Duplicate (top-right) to copy it into your own Notion workspace.
2
Create an integration — go to notion.so/my-integrations → New integration → name it "Dashboard" → copy the Internal Integration Secret.
3
Connect it to your database — open your Trades database in Notion → click ··· (top right) → Add connections → select "Dashboard".
4
Get your database ID — copy the URL of your Trades database. The ID is the long string after the last / and before ?v=.
